  1. Disco Elysium

    i

    Disco Elysium has some of the best world building in any of the games I have played. The artstyle and the music also made the game very immersive which makes it my favorite game of all time.

  2. Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

    i

    Counter-Strike has a long history in its series but CS:GO took the best qualities of all of its predecessors and made it into a near perfect game. Having a competitive game with minimal randomness to mechanics allows for strategies to be perfected.

  3. Path of Exile 1

    i

    Path of Exile is one of the many Action RPGs that exist alongside Diablo, Torchlight, and many others. It's depth in game mechanics and progression systems makes it stand far beyond the others.

  4. Diablo 3

    i

    Diablo has been considered the best Action RPG since Diablo 2, but for me Diablo 3 got me into the genre and while the systems and mechanics are not as complex as Path of Exiles, I had many good memories playing this game.

  5. Bloons Tower Defense 6

    i

    Bloons Tower Defence 6 for some reason is one of those games I just open if I don't feel like playing anything else.
