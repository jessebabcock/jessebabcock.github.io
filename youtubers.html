    The Primeagen used to be a Netflix engineer and turned into a youtuber/streamer. He now does recreational programming for content along with tech news and now a podcast with other engineers called The Standup.

  2. The Pragmatic Engineer

    i

    The Pragmatic Engineer hosts a podcast that he invites people from all over the tech industry to talk with. I usually listen to this on my morning drives and the interviews he has are really well informed.

  3. Mental Outlaw

    i

    Mental Outlaw is a very privacy and security oriented person, typically having news about the more illegal side of the internet and how governments and security experts are able to find exploits.

  4. ForrestKnight

    i

    When I started to first learn computer science and was learning Java, Forrest Knight at the time was huge in the Java space of Youtube so I have been following him for a while. He mostly does update videos on languages or huge tech news.

  5. Low Level

    i

    Low Level is a security expert that makes videos on breaches or exploits that are found (and patched before uploading). He goes into detail how the exploits work and how to prevent them.
